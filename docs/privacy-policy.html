Privacy Policy

Freelance Timer by Loopspur LLC

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Freelance Timer is designed with your privacy as a priority. We believe your business data belongs to you and should stay on your device.

Data Storage

All data you create in Freelance Timer—including clients, projects, time entries, and invoices—is stored locally on your device. Your data is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

  • Clients: Stored locally on device
  • Projects: Stored locally on device
  • Time entries: Stored locally on device
  • Invoices: Stored locally on device
  • Settings: Stored locally on device

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data you enter into the app

Third-Party Services

Freelance Timer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device encryption and a secure lock screen to protect your data.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual entries within the app
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

Freelance Timer is intended for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
